Privacy Policy

Last updated · May 19, 2026

Information we collect

Qlick does not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or contact info). We do not require an account to use the app.

Third-party services (ads & analytics)

To provide rewarded features, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. This includes:

  • Google AdMob — Used to serve rewarded video ads. AdMob collects the Google Advertising ID (AD_ID) and device-specific information to show relevant ads and measure performance.
  • Google Play Services — Used for core app functionality and performance tracking.

For more information on how Google uses data, see policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Data storage

All user-specific settings (keycap colors, labels, and personal records) are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not synchronized with any external servers.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE — Used only to provide tactile haptic feedback.
  • INTERNET — Used only to fetch rewarded advertisements from Google AdMob.
  • AD_ID — Used by AdMob for ad personalization and measurement.

Children's privacy

Qlick is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

Consent (EEA and UK users only)

Users in the EEA/UK can revoke their ad consent at any time through the 'Privacy' menu in the app settings.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact Jason Lee at doridooribasco@gmail.com.
